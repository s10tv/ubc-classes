This section of the course is ONLINE- distance education. Your instructor and course support staff will contact you by email prior to and during the course. Where applicable, please visit this site for examination information: http://www.students.ubc.ca/coursesreg/exams/

In this course you will learn how to translate statements in English (or other languages like Korean or Farsi) into a formal language for symbolic logic. You will also learn how to assess these statements and groups of them for different logical properties like consistency and validity. The course will use a textbook and computer software package developed at Stanford University. This course is mathematical in its content. Assessment will be by computer-graded exercises. This section of the course has no class meeting times.
